Empowering the Next Generation of Digital Talent

Ada, National College for Digital Skills, is dedicated to nurturing diverse talent and preparing them for successful careers in the digital economy. With campuses in London Victoria and Manchester Ancoats, we offer a unique blend of academic rigour and practical, industry-led experience.

Whether through our specialist Sixth Form or our innovative Apprenticeship programmes, Ada provides a pathway for young people to develop cutting-edge digital skills, connect with leading tech companies, and become the innovators of tomorrow.

Industry-Led Curriculum

Our courses are co-designed with industry partners to ensure relevance and employability.

About Ada, National College for Digital Skills

Ada, National College for Digital Skills, was founded with a clear mission: to address the growing digital skills gap in the UK by educating and empowering a new generation of diverse talent. Since our establishment, we have been at the forefront of digital education, blending academic excellence with practical industry relevance.

Our Mission

To inspire and equip young people from all backgrounds with the digital skills they need to thrive in a rapidly evolving world, and to become the innovators and leaders of tomorrow.

Our Vision

To be the leading national college for digital skills, renowned for our inclusive approach, industry impact, and the exceptional talent we produce.

Our Values

  • Creativity: Fostering imaginative problem-solving and innovative thinking.
  • Collaboration: Encouraging teamwork and shared learning experiences.
  • Curiosity: Inspiring a lifelong passion for learning and exploration in digital fields.
  • Rigour: Upholding high academic standards and meticulous attention to detail.
  • Resilience: Building the strength and adaptability to overcome challenges in a dynamic industry.

Ada Sixth Form: Your Digital Future Starts Here

Our specialist Sixth Form, based at our vibrant London Victoria campus, offers a unique post-16 education experience focused on preparing students for university or high-level apprenticeships in the digital sector. We combine a rigorous academic curriculum with hands-on, project-based learning.

Curriculum Highlights

Students choose from a range of A-Levels and BTECs, including Computer Science, Maths, Further Maths, and Digital Production. Our curriculum is designed to build a strong foundation in computational thinking and practical application.

  • A-Level Computer Science
  • A-Level Mathematics & Further Mathematics
  • BTEC Extended Diploma in Digital Production
  • Project-Based Learning & Hackathons

Student Life & Support

Beyond academics, Ada offers a supportive and engaging environment. Students benefit from dedicated pastoral care, career guidance, and opportunities to participate in extracurricular activities and industry workshops.

  • Personalised Career Guidance
  • University Application Support
  • Industry Mentoring
  • Student Societies & Events

Digital Apprenticeships: Learn, Earn, and Grow

Ada's Apprenticeship programmes provide an unparalleled opportunity to gain a qualification, earn a salary, and kickstart a career in tech. Available in both London Victoria and Manchester Ancoats, our apprenticeships combine on-the-job training with expert tuition.

Our Apprenticeship Programmes

We offer a range of Level 3 and Level 4 apprenticeships in high-demand digital roles, ensuring our apprentices develop skills directly applicable to the modern tech industry.

  • Software Development Apprenticeship
  • Data Analyst Apprenticeship
  • IT Support Technician Apprenticeship
  • Digital Marketing Apprenticeship

Partnering with Industry Leaders

Ada collaborates with a wide array of industry partners, from global tech giants to innovative startups, providing apprentices with invaluable experience and networking opportunities.

  • Access to Top Tech Companies
  • Mentorship from Industry Professionals
  • Guaranteed Work Experience
  • Pathways to Permanent Employment

Our Campuses: Hubs of Digital Innovation

Ada operates from two state-of-the-art campuses, strategically located in key digital hubs across the UK. Both sites provide modern learning environments equipped with cutting-edge technology and facilities designed to foster creativity and collaboration.

London Victoria Campus

Our London Victoria campus is home to our specialist Sixth Form and a thriving Apprenticeship provision. Located in the heart of London, it offers excellent transport links and proximity to many leading tech companies.

  • Modern Classrooms & Labs
  • Dedicated Student Hubs
  • Close to Tech & Creative Industries
  • Excellent Public Transport Access

Manchester Ancoats Campus

Situated in the vibrant Ancoats area, Manchester's digital and creative quarter, our Manchester campus focuses on delivering high-quality Apprenticeship programmes. It's a dynamic environment, surrounded by innovative businesses.

  • Industry-Standard Facilities
  • Collaborative Project Spaces
  • Part of Manchester's Digital Ecosystem
  • Strong Links with Northern Tech Firms
